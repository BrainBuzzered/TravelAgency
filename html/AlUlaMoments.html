ALULA ARABIAN LEOPARD WEEK

The first annual Arabian Leopard Week is inspired by the world's smallest big cat. These majestic creatures symbolise strength, beauty and elegance. With events across AlUla, we're raising awareness for these Critically Endangered cats.

ALULA ARTS FESTIVAL

Returning for its second edition, this unique festival brings together curated contemporary art, large-scale installations throughout the landscape, community murals, workshops, talks and more.

ALULA TRAIL RACE 2023

The AlUla Trail Race returns to challenge international athletes, amateur runners, and children alike. From a 100km ultramarathon, a 50km marathon, a 25km half marathon, a 10km fun run, and a 1.4km kids' race, there is something for everyone.
